Past Experiences


Adventures

Overseas Student Exchange Programme

I was lucky to get chosen to experience a student exchange held in Aalborg, Denmark. It's no doubt my favourite experience in ITE! We were there for 2 weeks to work on a networking project with the students of Tech College. Of course it wasn't all work, we had tons fun and time to explore the cities and even had a day trip to Amsterdam.

Graduation Day

Graduation day! I was blessed to have a very patient and caring lecturer during my fruitful 2 years of ITE. I was given endless opportunities from overseas trips to overseas competitions. I was honoured to receive the Innovation and Enterprise award during our graduation ceremony!

Lee Kuan Yew Technology Award

The LKY Technology Award is presented to students who have developed excellent projects that represent quality, technical innovation and creativity. My team and i attained Gold medal with our self-made application GrabHelp, which helps users locate nearest SOS devices in times of emergencies.

International Exhibition for Young Inventors

This competition was held in Indonesia back in 2019. I was unable to be there with my team, given that i was already given a chance to experience an overseas student exchange programme. My team represented ITE with our self made application, GrabHelp, and was awarded a Gold Medal and a Certificate of Recognition from the Philippine judges!

Internship at NCS

Students from ITE have to go through a 6 months internship. I got to work with NCS as a sever engineer. I was assigned to a project manager to aid him with setting up servers for an upcoming project. I got to explore what it's like to be a server engineer and had a little taste of how the things taught in school can be applied to real world situations.

Having fun while learning

The best way to learn is to enjoy and have fun while constantly learning. ITE have made my learning journey a very enjoyable one. Of course, my friends and lecturers have played a big part in this experience and i am very thankful for them. When we work hard, we play harder!
