SP Experiences

Technology is everywhere, more so in SP's School of Computing (SOC). You'll be amazed by the amount of knowledge there is in SOC. Learn not just from your tutors and lecturers, but also your classmates! Make the most out of your laptop here as you go through various computing modules.

I've only been through my first semester and I have already learnt how to build websites and applications. You can only imagine how much more there is to accomplish. The tech journey has been eye-opening so far for me, and I am sure SOC will have plenty in store for you!
